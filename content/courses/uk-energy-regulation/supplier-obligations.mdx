Energy suppliers have obligations to support energy efficiency.

## ECO (Energy Company Obligation)

Suppliers must fund/deliver efficiency to eligible households: insulation, heating upgrades, heating controls.

For business (non-residential), ECO has minimal direct impact. But the regulatory environment encourages suppliers to improve customer efficiency, creating market opportunities for ESCOs (energy service companies).

## Future trends

Likely to see more supplier-backed programs for business, following residential ECO model.

Current focus: SECR (reporting) and CCL/CCA (financial incentive).

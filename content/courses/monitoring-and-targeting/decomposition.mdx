**Decomposition** answers: "Energy up 15%. How much was real savings vs. weather/occupancy?"

Without it, you might claim a win that didn't happen.

## Framework

Total change = Structural (efficiency improvement) + Usage (occupancy/production) + Weather + Other

## Example

April 2024: 5,000 kWh. April 2025: 5,400 kWh (+8%).

Weather: 2°C colder → ~100 kWh increase expected.
Occupancy: +10%, but not energy-proportional.
Structural: LED retrofit (estimated -200 kWh/month).

**Decomposition:** 5,400 = 5,000 + 100 (weather) + 300 (occupancy) − 200 (LED) + 200 (other)

**True improvement: −200 kWh** (LEDs worked! Raw +8% hides a real win.)

## Methods

1. Regression (fit before/after, compare)
2. Degree-day adjustment
3. End-use data (if submeters exist)

Most use #1 or #2.

**Exception reporting** flags when consumption is abnormally high.

## The rule

Compare actual to expected (based on baseline and drivers like weather).

If actual > expected by >5–10%, flag and investigate.

## Example

Baseline: 7,500 kWh/month at 100 HDD.
October: 35 HDD → expected ≈ 2,625 kWh.
October actual: 2,850 kWh (8.5% high).

**Investigate:** Chiller running? Controls working? Radiator valve stuck? Equipment left on?

Often you find: zone heated AND cooled simultaneously, fan running 24/7 when it should be off nights, or boiler at 70°C instead of 55°C.

## Threshold & turnaround

Start at 10% threshold; refine after months. The faster you catch and fix, the less energy wasted. Real-time dashboards are most valuable.
